Human Rights & Labor Statuses

Study Area: SunPower Corp Manufacturing Locations

These maps explore various conditions of the countries in which SunPower owns and operates their manufacturing plants.


Human Rights

A measure of how well a government protects its citizen's physical integrity. This takes into account torture, government killing, political imprisonment, extrajudicial executions, mass killings, and disappearances. Scaled with a standard deviation below zero has less protection and above means more.


Human Rights Violations

Multiple variables are measured including press freedom, civil liberties, political freedoms, human trafficking, political prisoners, incarceration, religious persecution, torture and executions.


Economic Freedom

This variable is calculated by the degree to which individuals are free to choose, trade, and cooperate with others, and compete.


Wage per Hour in US$

Wage per hour calculated in US dollars.


Ratio of Female to Male Labor

This is measured by dividing the labor force of participating women by the that of the participating force of men. Labor force participation is defined by the proportion of population ages 15+ that are economically active.


Data was adapted from Our World in Data - Human Rights. CSV data was downloaded and adapted to fit the criteria of this project.
