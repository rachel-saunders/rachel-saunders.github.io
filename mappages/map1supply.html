Supply Chain

Study Area: Solar Star Farm California

This map explores the complex interactions and movement of products in the SunPower Corporation supply chain to assess the global reach of solar cells. For the purpose of this project an installation site location was picked to then trace the flow of materials from raw material sourcing to this installation site, the Solar Star in Rosemand California. It should be noted that this map is not nearly as complex as the actual supply chain for SunPower Corporation, but due to documentation retrictions and avaliable information this supply chain represents a basic supply chain structue. Also, the specific quartz and copper mines located in this map represent the top 5 exporting countries for these prodcuts. Due to a lack of avaliable information specific quartz and copper mines associated with the production of SunPower cells was not avaliable.
